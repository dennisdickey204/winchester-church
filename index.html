What We Believe

The Bible is the only word of God.

  • II Timothy 3:16 "All scripture is given by inspiriation of God, and is profitable for doctrine, repoof, correction, and instruction in righteousness

Jesus words will judge our soul.

  • John 12:48 He that rejects Me, and does not receive My words has that which judges him. The word that I have spoken will judge him in the last day."

Jesus the Christ died for us.

  • 1 Cor 15:3-4 "…Christ died for our sins according to the Scriptures, and He was buried and raised on the third day according to the Scriptures…"

Jesus Christ desires all to obey His gospel of faith, repentance, confession, baptism, and faithfulness.

  • Matthew 28:19 "Go therefore and make disciples of all the nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and the Son and the Holy Spirit,"
